🇶🇦 GENERAL IMMIGRATION PROCESS IN QATAR (DETAILED)

Qatar immigration is mainly controlled by the Ministry of Interior (MOI) and Ministry of Labour (MOL). The process follows a clear step-by-step structure.

1. 🎯 STEP 1: IDENTIFY YOUR PURPOSE

SHORT-TERM ENTRY:

  • Tourist Visa, Visa on Arrival, eVisa, Business Visa, Transit Visa, Short-term Entry Visa.

LONG-TERM STAY:

  • Work Visa, Family Residence, Student Visa, Investor Visa, Domestic Worker, Long-Term Residence/PR.

2. 🧾 STEP 2: CHECK ELIGIBILITY

Authorities verify Passport validity (min 6 months), Nationality eligibility, Profession, Financial stability, and Sponsor availability.

3. 🤝 STEP 3: ARRANGE SPONSOR (IF REQUIRED)

Visa TypeSponsor
Work VisaEmployer (Company)
Family VisaResident family member
Student VisaUniversity
Business VisaQatar company
Domestic WorkerHousehold employer
Investor VisaSelf / Company
Tourist / eVisaSometimes not required

4. 📄 STEP 4: PREPARE DOCUMENTS

Common documents include Passport, Photos, Application form, Tickets, Accommodation proof, Bank statements, Contracts, and Medical/Police certificates for long-term visas.

5. 🌐 STEP 5: APPLY FOR VISA

🟢 1. ONLINE (EVISA SYSTEM)

Official portal: www.hayya.qa (Tourist, Business, GCC, and Short-term visas).

🟡 2. EMPLOYER / SPONSOR APPLICATION

Handled by MOL and MOI for Work, Residence, Family, and Investor visas.

🔵 3. EMBASSY / CONSULATE

Used for special cases or non-eVisa eligible nationalities.

6. 💳 STEP 6: PAY VISA FEES

Payment varies: Online for eVisa, Employer pays for Work visas, Sponsor pays for Family visas.

7. 🔍 STEP 7: APPLICATION REVIEW

Verification by MOI (Immigration), MOL (Labour), and MOFA (Diplomatic cases) for identity, security, and medical eligibility.

8. ✅ STEP 8: VISA APPROVAL & ISSUANCE

If approved, an eVisa is sent via email, or a Work entry permit is issued. Residence visas are linked to the Qatar ID (QID).

9. ✈️ STEP 9: TRAVEL TO QATAR

Upon arrival, biometrics are taken, the entry stamp is issued, and the visa is activated.

10. 🏠 STEP 10: RESIDENCE REGISTRATION

For long-term visas: Mandatory Medical test, Fingerprint biometrics, QID issuance, and Residence Permit (RP) activation.

11. 📌 STEP 11: STAY RULES & COMPLIANCE

Follow visa purpose strictly. No illegal work on visit visas. Renew QID on time. Overstay leads to fines and deportation.

12. 🔁 STEP 12: RENEWAL / EXIT / CHANGE STATUS

Includes visa renewal, sponsorship transfer (with approval), or status change inside Qatar.
