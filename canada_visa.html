🇨🇦 CANADA IMMIGRATION — MASTER END-TO-END PROCESS (2026)

🟢 PHASE 1: IMMIGRATION PATHWAY SELECTION (CORE SYSTEM)

Canada immigration is divided into 4 major streams:

1. TEMPORARY RESIDENCE (ENTRY VISAS)

  • Visitor Visa (TRV)
  • Study Permit
  • Work Permit (LMIA / Open Work Permit)
  • Super Visa (parents/grandparents)

👉 Purpose: Temporary stay, entry into Canada

2. PERMANENT RESIDENCE (PR)

  • Express Entry (FSW / CEC / FST)
  • Provincial Nominee Program (PNP)
  • Atlantic Immigration Program (AIP)
  • Rural & Northern Immigration Pilot (RNIP)
  • Caregiver pathways

👉 Purpose: Permanent settlement

3. FAMILY SPONSORSHIP & HUMANITARIAN

  • Spouse/common-law, Parents, Grandparents, and Dependent children sponsorship.
  • Refugee status and Asylum claims inside Canada.

🟡 PHASE 2: ELIGIBILITY SCREENING (CANADA CORE FILTER)

IRCC checks the following:

  • 1. Identity: Valid passport and biometrics registration.
  • 2. Purpose: Alignment with Study, Work, PR, or Family sponsorship.
  • 3. Language: IELTS General / CELPIP (English) or TEF (French) for PR points.
  • 4. Education: ECA (Educational Credential Assessment) for foreign degrees.
  • 5. Financial Proof: Tuition + living for students, travel for visitors, or settlement funds for PR.
  • 6. Medical & Security: Panel physician exams, police clearance (PCC), and background screening.

🟠 PHASE 3: DOCUMENT PREPARATION (CORE FILE)

  • Identity: Passport and photos (IRCC format).
  • Civil: Birth and marriage certificates.
  • Financial: 4–6 months bank statements, income proof, or sponsor letters.
  • Education: Degrees, transcripts, and mandatory ECA report.
  • Work: Reference letters (NOC/TEER coding), job offers, and contracts.
  • Immigration specific: LMIA, PNP nomination, or Invitation to Apply (ITA).

🔵 PHASE 4 & 5: APPLICATION & BIOMETRICS

1. Create IRCC account (GCKey) -> 2. Fill online forms -> 3. Upload documents -> 4. Pay fees -> 5. Submit -> 6. Attend biometrics at VFS Global India.

🟤 PHASE 6 & 7: IRCC ASSESSMENT & CHECKS

Evaluations: Completeness check, immigration history, LMIA validation (Work), ECA validation (Education), source of funds, and criminal admissibility. May include Additional Document Requests (ADR) or medical re-checks.

🟠 PHASE 8, 9 & 10: DECISION, APPROVAL & ENTRY

Outcomes: Approved or Refused. Approval grants permits or COPR (Confirmation of PR). Entry: CBSA inspection and biometric verification at the Canadian border.

🟣 PHASE 11 & 12: LIVING IN CANADA & PR PATHWAYS

Compliance: Follow permit conditions (work limits/residency obligations). PR Routes: Express Entry (Profile -> CRS score -> ITA -> PR), PNP (+600 points), Study → PGWP → PR, or Work → PR.

🟢 PHASE 13 & 14: PR OBLIGATIONS & CITIZENSHIP

PR Obligations: 730 days residency in 5 years and tax filing. Citizenship: 3 years physical presence in 5 years + Language test + Citizenship test + Oath ceremony.

⚫ MASTER FLOW (CANADA IMMIGRATION SYSTEM MAP)

Eligibility → IELTS + ECA → Application (IRCC portal) → Biometrics → IRCC processing → Decision → Entry into Canada → Temporary stay → PR application (Express Entry / PNP) → PR approval (COPR) → Residency → Citizenship
