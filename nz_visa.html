🇳🇿 NEW ZEALAND VISA SYSTEM — MASTER END-TO-END PROCESS (2026)

🟢 PHASE 1: IDENTIFY VISA PATHWAY (SELECTION STAGE)

Every applicant first goes through visa classification selection.

1. Determine purpose of entry:

You must clearly choose ONE:

  • Visitor (tourism / family visit)
  • Study (education pathway)
  • Work (temporary employment)
  • Residence (permanent migration)
  • Business / Investment
  • Family (partner/child/parent)
  • Protection / Refugee
  • Transit / Short stay
  • Special categories (RSE, Talent, Religious, etc.)

2. Match visa category:

Temporary visas: Visitor Visa, NZeTA, Student Visa, Post Study Work Visa, AEWV, Specific Purpose Work Visa, Working Holiday Visa, RSE Limited Visa, Interim Visa, Transit Visa, Religious Worker Visa, Global Impact Visa, Talent Work Visa.

Residence visas: Skilled Migrant Category Resident Visa, Green List Straight to Residence, Green List Work to Residence, Partner Resident Visa, Parent Resident Visa, Dependent Child Resident Visa, Entrepreneur Resident Visa, Investor Visa, Talent Resident Visa, Legacy categories.

Protection visas: Refugee & Protected Person Visa, Claimant Visa.

🟡 PHASE 2: ELIGIBILITY CHECK (CORE SCREENING)

Immigration NZ checks 5 universal pillars:

  • 1. Identity: Valid passport, Legal name consistency, Nationality verification.
  • 2. Health requirement: Medical exam (x-ray + blood tests), Must not pose public health risk.
  • 3. Character requirement: Police clearance certificates (all countries lived in 12+ months), No serious criminal record.
  • 4. Genuine intent: Must match visa purpose (Study intent → student visa, Job intent → work visa, Settlement intent → residence visa).
  • 5. Financial capability: Proof depends on visa type (Visitor → daily funds, Student → tuition + living cost, Work → job income, Residence → settlement funds / investment).

🟠 PHASE 3: DOCUMENT PREPARATION (UNIVERSAL CORE FILE)

Almost ALL visas require: Identity documents (Passport valid 6–24 months, Photos), Civil documents (Birth/Marriage certificates), Financial documents (Bank statements 3–6 months, Income tax returns, Sponsorship), Medicals, and Police clearance (India PCC + any country lived in).

🔵 PHASE 4: APPLICATION SUBMISSION

  • 1. Create Immigration New Zealand account
  • 2. Select visa category
  • 3. Fill application form (online)
  • 4. Upload documents
  • 5. Pay visa fee
  • 6. Submit application

🟣 PHASE 5: IMMIGRATION ASSESSMENT PROCESS

This is the core decision-making stage involving Application triage, Background checks, Employer verification, Education validation, Relationship verification, Financial verification, and Risk assessment.

🟤 PHASE 6 & 7: INTERVIEW & DECISION STAGE

May include Immigration interview (phone/video/in-person). Outcomes: Visa granted (approved), Declined (refused), or Further information required.

🟢 PHASE 8: VISA GRANT CONDITIONS

If approved, visa includes Validity period and Conditions (Employer-specific, job role restricted, salary threshold, or work limits).

🔵 PHASE 9 & 10: ENTRY & POST-ARRIVAL COMPLIANCE

On arrival: Immigration border check. Compliance: Must follow visa conditions, maintain status, and report changes (job, address, partner).

🟡 PHASE 11, 12 & 13: TRANSITION, PR & CITIZENSHIP

Temporary → Residence pathways → Permanent Resident Visa (PR after 2 years) → New Zealand citizenship (usually after 5 years residence).

⚫ UNIVERSAL FLOW (SIMPLIFIED MASTER MAP)

Eligibility → Documents → Application → Assessment → Decision → Visa Grant → Entry → Compliance → Extension/Transition → Residence → Permanent Residency → Citizenship
