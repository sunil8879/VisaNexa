Australia PR: DIY vs Agency

1. End-to-End Immigration Lifecycle
Technical Glossary:

• ACS: Australian Computer Society (Assesses IT Professionals).
• EA: Engineers Australia (Assesses Engineering degrees/experience).
• SkillSelect: The Govt. platform where EOIs are ranked for invitations.
• EOI: Expression of Interest (Your digital "hand-raise" in the points pool).
• ImmiAccount: The official Department of Home Affairs portal for visa filing.

🏢 Agency Workflow

Step 1: Profile Audit & SOL Check Agency checks if your job is on the Skilled Occupation List (SOL). They use internal software to estimate points. Time: 2-3 Days
Step 2: CV & Letter Drafting Agency "re-writes" your CV and drafts "Service Requirement Letters." They ensure keywords match Australian ANZSCO codes. Time: 1 Week
Step 3: English Exam Referral They push you toward partnered coaching centers for IELTS/PTE. They manage your booking for you. Time: 2-4 Weeks
Step 4: Skill Assessment Filing The agency uploads your degrees to ACS or EA. You pay the fee to the agency, and they pay the body. Time: 8-12 Weeks
Step 5: EOI Pool Entry They create a SkillSelect profile for you. You do not get the login; they manage it to keep you "dependent." Time: 1 Day (Once assessed)
Step 6: State Nomination (ROI) They apply for State/Regional nomination (NSW, VIC, QLD). They monitor state portals for "Registration of Interest" windows. Time: 3-6 Months
Step 7: Final Visa Submission Once invited, they file the visa on ImmiAccount. They handle the "decision-ready" document checklist. Time: 2 Weeks
Step 8: RFI & Follow-up They respond to "Request for Information" (RFI) from the Case Officer and track the status. Time: Until Decision

🧾 DIY Workflow

Step 1: SOL Self-Verification You go to Home Affairs and check your ANZSCO code and occupation eligibility yourself. Time: 1 Day
Step 2: CV Formatting (Australian Standard) You format your CV to be 2-3 pages, chronological, focus on technical "Tasks Performed" as per ANZSCO requirements. Time: 2-3 Days
Step 3: Direct Exam Booking You book directly via IELTS.org or PTE.com. No middleman markup. Time: 2-4 Weeks
Step 4: Direct Skill Assessment You create a login on ACS or EA websites. You upload your PDFs directly. You own the tracking ID. Time: 8-12 Weeks
Step 5: SkillSelect Ownership You create your own EOI profile. You have the password. You can update your points (like new English score) any time. Time: 1 Day
Step 6: State Nomination Submission You apply directly to State websites (e.g., NSW.gov.au). You receive the nomination email directly. Time: 3-6 Months
Step 7: Direct ImmiAccount Filing You fill the visa form on the IMMI portal. You pay the fee using your own credit card. No "hidden service fees." Time: 2-3 Days
Step 8: Personal Monitoring You get the Case Officer’s email directly. You upload Medicals and PCC (Police Clearance) yourself. Time: Until Decision
The Hard Truth:
Whether you pay an agent ₹20 Lakhs or do it yourself for ₹4 Lakhs, you are using the exact same ACS, EA, SkillSelect, and IMMI portals. The Australian Government does not give priority to applications submitted by agents.

SYSTEM REALITY: The Australian Govt. Portal does not distinguish between an Agent and an Individual.

4. Risk Assessment

DIY RISKS

• Minor administrative errors in forms.

• Slower learning curve of the portal.

• Wrong occupation code selection.

FINANCIAL RISK: LOW (Only Govt fees at stake)

AGENCY RISKS

• Total loss of ₹20 Lacs if no invitation arrives.

• Mis-selling: "Guaranteed PR" lies.

• Document Forgery: Leading to a 10-year ban.

• Refund Issues: Most contracts are non-refundable.

FINANCIAL RISK: EXTREMELY HIGH
