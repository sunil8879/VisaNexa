France Immigration: DIY vs AGENCY

🚨 THE FRENCH LABOUR MARKET REALITY

France has a highly protective labour market. For most standard work visas, an employer must prove to DREETS that they couldn't find a French or EU candidate. Agencies often charge ₹10L+ promising to "skip" this rule—in reality, if the government finds the job offer isn't genuine, your visa is rejected and you are banned from the Schengen area.

1. End-to-End Immigration Lifecycle
Technical Glossary:

• Passeport Talent: A special 4-year visa for high-skilled workers (IT, Scientists, Artists).
• OFII: French Office for Immigration and Integration (The body that validates your arrival).
• France-Visas: The single official portal for all visa applications.
• VLS-TS: Long-stay visa equivalent to a residence permit for the first year.

🧾 DIY Workflow

Step 1: Direct Job HuntYou apply via Welcome to the Jungle, HelloWork, or LinkedIn.Time: 2-6 Months
Step 2: DIY French CVYou create a professional CV using free tools like Canva or Europass. You include your English/French levels.Time: 1 Day
Step 3: Direct InterviewYou clear technical rounds with a real French company HR. The job offer is 100% genuine and free.Time: 2-4 Weeks
Step 4: Official Work PermitYour employer files for your authorization via the ANEF Portal.Time: 4-6 Weeks
Step 5: Direct Visa FilingYou fill the application yourself on France-Visas. You own the tracking ID and login.Time: 1 Week
Step 6: Direct VFS StampingYou book your own appointment at VFS Global. You pay only the official ₹9,000 fee.Time: 1-2 Weeks
Step 7: Travel BookingYou choose your airline and travel to Paris, Lyon, or Marseille. You are a free professional.Time: 1 Week
Step 8: Self-Managed OFIIYou validate your VLS-TS visa online via the OFII website yourself.Time: 2 Days

3. Timeline & Critical Risks

TIMELINE

• Job Hunting: 3-8 months (French firms are slow recruiters)

• Work Permit: 2 months (Ministry backlog)

• Visa Stamping: 15 working days

Note: No agency can "speed up" the OFII or DREETS decision. If an agent promises a visa in 15 days, they are likely giving you a fake document.

CRITICAL RISKS

• Language Trap: Promising a job without French (B1/B2) for non-IT roles is a scam.

• Cerfa Fraud: Agents providing forged job contracts leading to a lifetime Schengen ban.

• Financial Loss: Paying lakhs for a job in a company that goes bankrupt before you land.

4. What Actually Decides Success?

2. Talent Passport CriteriaIf you earn €42,000+ or have a Masters, you bypass the "Labour Market Test." No agent is needed for this.
