🇷🇺 GENERAL IMMIGRATION PROCESS – RUSSIA (2026)

🧭 1. DECIDE YOUR PURPOSE

Select your category: Tourist (L), Business (M), Work (Z), Student (X), or Settlement (TRP/PR). This decides if you need an official Invitation or can use the E-Visa.

📩 2. OBTAIN BASIS DOCUMENT

Most Russian visas require a formal "Invitation":

  • Tourist: Voucher from a hotel or agency.
  • Student: Admission letter from a Russian university.
  • Work: Official invitation from the employer (GUVM).

📄 3 & 4. APPLICATION & ENTRY

Fill the online form and submit at the Embassy or VFS Global. Once approved, travel with your passport and the Arrival Card (issued on the flight).

🏠 5. MIGRATION REGISTRATION (CRITICAL)

Mandatory: Within 7 working days of arrival, you must register your stay through your hotel or landlord. Failure to do this leads to fines and deportation.

📑 6 & 7. TEMPORARY RESIDENCE (TRP)

The first step for long-term stay. Requirements: Medical tests (HIV, TB), no criminal record, and often a quota approval. Validity: 3 years.

🏠 8, 9 & 10. PR & CITIZENSHIP

After 6–12 months on TRP, apply for Permanent Residence (PR). After 5 years of PR, you are eligible for Russian Citizenship (requires language proficiency).

🧠 SUMMARY FLOW: Choose Visa → Get Invitation → VFS Submission → Stamping → Entry → 7-Day Registration → TRP → PR → Citizenship
