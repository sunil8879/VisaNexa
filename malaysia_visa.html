🇲🇾 GENERAL PROCESS FOR MALAYSIA IMMIGRATION

🧭 1. IDENTIFY PURPOSE & CATEGORY

Decide your entry reason: Tourist (Social Visit), Work (Employment Pass), Study (Student Pass via EMGS), or Long-term (MM2H). This decides your visa type and document requirements.

📄 2 & 3. CHECK VISA & SPONSORSHIP

Check if you need an eVisa or eNTRI. For Work or Study, your employer or university must apply for your approval through the ESD or EMGS portals first.

📑 4 & 5. SUBMISSION & APPROVAL

Submit documents: Passport (6+ months), photos, offer letters, and financial proof. The Immigration Department reviews background, security, and employer validity. Approval is usually issued electronically.

✈️ 6. ENTRY INTO MALAYSIA

At the airport, present your passport and visa approval. The immigration officer verifies eligibility and issues your entry stamp.

🛡️ 7. POST-ARRIVAL REQUIREMENTS

  • Medical: Mandatory check-up for workers/students.
  • Endorsement: Collecting your physical pass sticker or card at the immigration office.
  • Registration: Reporting presence to EMGS or the employer.

🔄 8. STAY, RENEWAL & EXIT

Renew passes before expiry. Ensure compliance with stay rules. Overstaying results in heavy fines and possible entry bans from Malaysia.
