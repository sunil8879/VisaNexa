USA H-1B: DIY vs AGENCY TRUTH

🚨 THE US REALITY CHECK

Unlike Australia, you cannot "apply" for a US Work Visa yourself. You MUST have a US Employer. Agencies exploit this by "selling" you job spots from shady consultancies.

1. Detailed Immigration Lifecycle
Technical Glossary:

• USCIS: US Citizenship and Immigration Services (The body that approves petitions).
• LCA: Labor Condition Application (Employer must prove they pay you the prevailing wage).
• Lottery: The random selection process for H-1B (Selection chance ~20-30%).
• DS-160: The official online form for your visa interview appointment.

🏢 Agency Workflow

Step 1: Profile MarketingAgency evaluates if they can "sell" your profile to a US-based 'Desi' consultancy.Time: 1 Week
Step 2: CV MaskingThey rewrite your CV, often inflating experience or adding keywords to bypass US applicant tracking systems.Time: 1-2 Weeks
Step 3: The "Placement"They connect you to a middleman employer in the US who agrees to file your H-1B for a bribe.Time: 1-3 Months
Step 4: LCA FilingThe agency handles the LCA via the employer. You are rarely shown the actual salary mentioned.Time: 7-10 Days
Step 5: Lottery RegistrationEmployer registers you in the March H-1B cap lottery. Agency charges you "Registration Fees."Time: March (Annual)
Step 6: Petition FilingIf selected, they file the I-129 petition. They often use 'Premium Processing' to justify more fees.Time: April - June
Step 7: DS-160 & Mock InterviewsAgency fills your DS-160 and "coaches" you on what to say to the officer to hide the bribe.Time: 2-3 Weeks
Step 8: Port of Entry GuidanceThey tell you what to carry in your bags to avoid being sent back by CBP at the airport.Time: 1 Day

🧾 DIY Workflow

Step 1: Direct Job HuntingYou search for H-1B sponsoring companies via H1B Grader or LinkedIn.Time: 2-6 Months
Step 2: US-Standard CV PrepYou format your CV to be 1-page (US style), focusing on quantifiable achievements and tech stack.Time: 3-5 Days
Step 3: Direct InterviewsYou crack technical rounds with Tier-1/Tier-2 US companies. The job offer is 100% genuine.Time: 1-2 Months
Step 4: Official LCA ProcessThe company HR files LCA via DOL.gov. This is legally required to be paid by the employer.Time: 1 Week
Step 5: Lottery EntryThe company Attorney enters you into the lottery. Legally, the $10 fee is paid by the employer.Time: March
Step 6: USCIS PetitionIf selected, company lawyers handle all USCIS.gov filings. You pay ₹0 for this.Time: 15-90 Days
Step 7: Direct Visa BookingYou fill DS-160 on State.gov and pay the $205 fee yourself.Time: 2 Weeks
Step 8: Travel & OnboardingYou fly to the US and start at the actual office of the company that hired you.Time: 1 Day

3. Time & Critical Risks

TIMELINE

• Job Hunting: 3-12 months (Continuous)

• Lottery Cycle: Fixed (March Entry / October Start)

• Visa Stamping: 2-4 weeks

Note: No agency can bypass the March lottery. If you miss it, you wait another year.

AGENCY FRAUD RISKS

• Bench Scams: You have a visa but no work or salary.

• Fraudulent LCA: Misreporting salary to USCIS.

• 214(b) Refusal: Permanent ban for misrepresentation.

• No Refund: Most agencies disappear after a lottery rejection.
