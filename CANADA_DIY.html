Canada PR: DIY vs AGENCY TRUTH

🚨 THE AUTOMATED SYSTEM REALITY

Canada's Express Entry is a purely mathematical algorithm. It ranks you based on your CRS Score. No agency has a "quota" or "special link" with IRCC. If your score is 430 and the cutoff is 500, no amount of money paid to an agent in Mumbai can get you an invitation. You are paying lakhs for someone to fill a form you can do for free.

1. End-to-End Immigration Lifecycle
Technical Glossary:

• IRCC: Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada (The only official body).
• CRS Score: Comprehensive Ranking System (The points that decide your rank).
• ECA (WES): Educational Credential Assessment (Verifies your Indian degree vs Canada standards).
• ITA: Invitation to Apply (The golden ticket to file your PR documents).

4. What Actually Decides Success in Canada?
1. CLB 9 in IELTSScoring 8,7,7,7 is the "Magic Number." It adds huge points that no agent can provide.
2. Age BracketMax points are given to those between 20-29. Every year after 30, you lose points.
3. STEM/Healthcare CategoryIRCC now holds "Category-Based Draws." Doctors, Nurses, and Engineers are selected at lower scores.
4. Masters DegreeA 1-year PG Diploma or 2-year Masters from India gives you the crucial points needed to cross the 500 mark.
