🇸🇬 GENERAL PROCESS FOR SINGAPORE IMMIGRATION

🧭 STEP 1 & 2: IDENTIFY PURPOSE & CHOOSE PASS

First decide your immigration category and match it to the correct pass:

  • Tourism: Short-Term Visit Pass (STVP).
  • Study: Student Pass.
  • Work: Employment Pass (EP), S Pass, or Work Permit.
  • Business: EntrePass or ONE Pass.
  • Investment: Global Investor Programme (GIP).
  • Family: Dependant Pass, LTVP, or LTVP+.
  • Permanent Residency: PR via PTS, Family, or GIP schemes.

📝 STEP 3: SECURE CORE REQUIREMENTS

Depending on category, obtain your basis for entry: University admission for students, a job offer from a Singapore employer for workers, or a business plan for entrepreneurs.

📑 STEP 4 & 5: ONLINE SUBMISSION & DOCUMENTS

Applications are submitted via ICA, MOM, or EDB portals. Standard documents include: Passport, photos, offer/admission letters, financial proof, education certificates, and medical reports if requested.

💳 STEP 6 & 7: FEES & PROCESSING

Pay application fees (SGD 30–225 typical). Wait times vary: Visit visas (1–3 days), Work/Study passes (2–8 weeks), PR/Investor visas (6–12 months).

🛬 STEP 8 & 9: APPROVAL, ENTRY & COLLECTION

Receive your In-Principle Approval (IPA) or visa. Travel to Singapore, clear immigration, and complete biometrics to collect your physical pass card (Student Pass, EP, or PR confirmation).

🔁 STEP 10: RENEWAL & PR CONVERSION

Renew passes before expiry. After working or staying for the eligible number of years, you may apply for Permanent Residency (PR) through the ICA.

📊 SUMMARY: Choose Pass → Get Job/Admission → Apply Online → Wait Approval → Enter SG → Collect Pass → PR Option Later
