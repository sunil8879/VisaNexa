Russia Work Visa: DIY vs AGENCY TRUTH

🚨 THE RUSSIAN INVITATION REALITY

In Russia, the Work Invitation (Priglashenie) is a legal document issued only by the Ministry of Internal Affairs (MVD). Agencies often charge ₹3L to ₹8L for "Russia Job Packages"—in reality, they are just acting as brokers for companies looking for low-cost labor. If an agent provides a fake invitation, you will be detained at the airport and deported immediately.

1. End-to-End Immigration Lifecycle
Technical Glossary:

• MVD: Ministry of Internal Affairs (The core body for all migration controls).
• GUVM: Main Directorate for Migration Affairs (Issues work permits and registrations).
• Priglashenie: The official invitation letter required to apply for the visa in India.
• HIV Certificate: A mandatory medical document required for all Russian work visas.

THE "BUSINESS VISA" TRAP

• Work on Business Visa: Agents sending you on a Business visa to work. This is 100% illegal and leads to DEPORTATION.

• HIV Result Fraud: Providing fake medicals to bypass the strict Russian health laws.

• Registration Failure: Agents failing to register you within 7 days, making you an "Illegal Overstayer."
