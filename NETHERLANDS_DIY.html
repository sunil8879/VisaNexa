Netherlands KM: DIY vs AGENCY TRUTH

🚨 THE DUTCH SPONSORSHIP REALITY

In the Netherlands, you cannot "buy" a work permit. Most Indians enter as a Highly Skilled Migrant (Kennismigrant). Crucially, only companies registered as "Recognized Sponsors" with the IND can hire you. Agencies often charge ₹10L+ claiming they have "exclusive spots"—in reality, if the company isn't on the official IND list, they cannot give you a visa. You are paying for a job offer that is legally free.

1. End-to-End Immigration Lifecycle
Technical Glossary:

• IND: Immigration and Naturalisation Service (The body that approves all residency).
• Recognized Sponsor: A Dutch company pre-approved by the IND to hire foreign talent.
• MVV: The entry visa required to fly from India to the Netherlands.
• BSN: Your mandatory social security number issued by the local Dutch municipality.

🧾 DIY Workflow

Step 2: Clean Dutch CVYou create a professional, honest CV. You highlight your tech stack or niche domain experience.Time: 2 Days
Step 3: Direct InterviewYou clear technical rounds with a real Dutch company (Booking.com, ASML, Philips). The job is 100% genuine.Time: 2-4 Weeks

Step 5: Direct MVV CodeYour HR sends you the official approval letter via email. You pay ₹0 for this.Time: Instant

Step 8: Self-Managed RegistrationYou book your own appointment at the 'Expat Center' or Gemente yourself. You control your BSN.Time: 2 Weeks

3. Timeline & Critical Risks

TIMELINE

• Job Hunting: 3-6 months (Dutch hiring is very direct)

• IND Approval: 14-21 working days (Fastest in EU)

• Visa Stamping: 10 working days

Note: The Netherlands has a highly digitalized immigration system. No agency can "speed up" the IND decision. It is already built for speed.

THE "SALARY" RISKS

• Salary Inflation: Agent claims you earn €5,500 to get visa, but actually pays you €2,000. This is tax fraud.

• Non-Sponsor Trap: Agencies selling jobs in companies that don't have "Recognized Sponsor" status.

• Immediate Termination: If the IND finds the job is "purchased," you are deported within 24 hours.

4. What Actually Decides Success?
1. Salary ThresholdYou must meet the KM salary criteria (approx €5,331 for 30+; €3,909 for under 30). No agent can bypass this.
2. Recognized Sponsor ListYour target company MUST be on the public list. If they aren't, they cannot sponsor you. Always verify this at IND Public Register.
3. Technical DemandJava/Python Developers, Data Scientists, and Semiconductor Engineers (ASML) have 95% hiring success.
4. Direct HR RelationsDutch people value honesty and directness ("Poldermodel"). They prefer direct LinkedIn reaches over agent files.
