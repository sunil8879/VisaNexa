🇦🇪 GENERAL UAE VISA PROCESS (2026)

🧭 1. IDENTIFY VISA TYPE

First step is choosing the correct category:

  • Tourist / Visit visa → travel purpose
  • Work visa → job offer required
  • Student visa → university admission required
  • Investor / Golden visa → investment or talent proof
  • Family visa → sponsor in UAE required
  • Freelance / Remote visa → income or license required

👉 UAE visa system is purpose-based, not one single universal application.

📌 2. FIND SPONSOR / ELIGIBILITY BASE

Most UAE visas require a sponsor or eligibility base:

  • Employer (Work Visa)
  • University (Student Visa)
  • Family member (Family Visa)
  • Company / Free Zone (Business Visa)
  • Self-eligibility (Golden Visa / Investor / Freelance / Remote Work)
  • Airline / hotel / agent (Tourist Visa)

📄 3. DOCUMENT PREPARATION

Common documents across all visa types:

  • Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity)
  • Passport-size photograph (white background)
  • Completed application form
  • Proof of purpose (job offer / admission / investment / invitation)
  • Financial proof (if required)
  • Travel insurance (for most short-term visas)

🏛️ 4. VISA APPLICATION SUBMISSION

Applications are submitted through official channels:

  • ICP Portal (Federal UAE system)
  • GDRFA (Dubai Immigration)
  • MOHRE (Work visas)
  • Free Zone Authorities (Dubai Media City, DIFC, etc.)
  • UAE Embassy / Consulate (outside UAE)
  • Airlines / hotels (tourist visas in some cases)

💳 5. PAYMENT OF FEES

Fees depend on visa type (Tourist: low cost, Work: employer pays, Investor/Golden: higher). Additional charges may include Medical test, Emirates ID, Insurance, and Typing center fees.

🧪 6. MEDICAL FITNESS TEST

Mandatory for long-term visas (Work, Residence, Family, Student). Includes Blood test and Chest X-ray (TB screening) conducted in UAE-approved medical centers.

🪪 7. EMIRATES ID APPLICATION

After approval, biometric fingerprints, photo capture, and identity registration are required. Emirates ID is mandatory for all UAE residents.

🔍 8. IMMIGRATION / SECURITY VERIFICATION

Authorities verify passport authenticity, criminal background (home country + UAE checks), sponsor validity, and financial or employment status.

✅ 9 & 10. APPROVAL, ISSUANCE & ENTRY

If approved, an Entry Permit is issued for those outside. On arrival: immigration checkpoint verification, entry permit validation, and visa activation.

🏠 11. RESIDENCE ACTIVATION (LONG-TERM VISAS)

For resident visas: Visa stamped or e-residence issued, Emirates ID delivered, and health insurance activated (mandatory in most emirates).

🔁 12. RENEWAL OR STATUS CHANGE

Before expiry: Visa renewal application, medical test repeat, and Emirates ID renewal. Alternatively, visa conversion (e.g., Visit → Work → Residence).

❌ 13. VISA CANCELLATION / EXIT RULES

Visa ends when employer cancels, sponsor withdraws support, investment is sold, or upon expiry. Overstay leads to daily penalties + possible bans.

🧠 SIMPLE FLOW SUMMARY

Choose Visa Type → Meet Eligibility (Sponsor / Job / Investment / Study) → Submit Documents → Pay Fees → Medical Test (if required) → Biometrics (Emirates ID) → Immigration Approval → Visa Issuance → Entry / Residence Activation → Renewal or Extension

🌐 OFFICIAL UAE VISA PORTALS

1. ICP (Federal Immigration): icp.gov.ae

2. GDRFA Dubai: gdrfad.gov.ae

3. MOHRE (Work Permits): mohre.gov.ae
