🇴🇲 GENERAL IMMIGRATION PROCESS – OMAN (DETAILED)

Oman immigration is primarily controlled by the Royal Oman Police (ROP), Ministry of Labour (MOL), and MOCIIP.

🧭 STEP 1: IDENTIFY PURPOSE OF ENTRY

SHORT-TERM ENTRY:

  • Tourist Visa, eVisa, Visa on Arrival, Transit Visa, Business Visa, Short-Term Entry Visa.

LONG-TERM RESIDENCE:

  • Work Visa, Family Residence, Family Visit, Student Visa, Domestic Worker, Investor/Partner, Retirement, Long-Term Residence.

🧾 STEP 2: CHECK ELIGIBILITY

Authorities verify Passport validity (6 months), Nationality eligibility (for eVisa/VOA), Financial stability, Job offer/Sponsorship, and Criminal records.

🤝 STEP 3: ARRANGE SPONSOR (IF REQUIRED)

Visa TypeSponsor
Work VisaOmani Employer
Family VisaResident in Oman
Student VisaUniversity
Business VisaCompany in Oman
Domestic WorkerHousehold Employer
Investor VisaSelf / Business Entity
Tourist VisaUsually not required

📄 STEP 4: DOCUMENT PREPARATION

Common requirements: Passport, Photos, Application form, Job offer (Work), Admission letter (Student), Invitation (Business/Family), Medical/Police certificates.

🌐 STEP 5: VISA APPLICATION

🟢 A. ONLINE EVISA SYSTEM

Portal: evisa.rop.gov.om (Tourist, Business, GCC, and Short-term visas).

🟡 B. SPONSOR-BASED APPLICATION

Handled by Employer, Family, or University through the Royal Oman Police (ROP).

🔵 C. VISA ON ARRIVAL

Available at the airport for eligible countries after document checks.

💳 STEP 6 & 7: FEES & REVIEW

Fees are paid online or by sponsors. ROP verifies identity, security background, sponsor legitimacy, and medical eligibility for long-term stays.

✅ STEP 8 & 9: APPROVAL & ENTRY

Approved eVisas arrive by email. Entry includes immigration verification, biometric scans (if required), and entry stamp issuance.

🏠 STEP 10: RESIDENCE PROCESS (LONG-TERM)

  • 1. Medical examination (approved clinics)
  • 2. Biometric registration
  • 3. Residence Permit application
  • 4. Oman ID issuance
  • 5. Final residency activation

📌 STEP 11 & 12: STAY RULES & RENEWAL

Follow visa purpose strictly. Overstay leads to fines. Renewals occur every 1–2 years. Job transfers require labour approval.

🧠 SUMMARY FLOW: Purpose → Visa Type → Sponsor → Documents → Application → Review → Approval → Entry → Medical/Biometrics → Oman ID → Residency
