🇵🇱 GENERAL IMMIGRATION PROCESS FOR POLAND

🎯 PHASE 1: DECIDE PURPOSE

Identify your category: Work (Job Offer/Blue Card), Study (University), Business (Entrepreneur), or Family (Spouse/Child). This decides if you need a National Visa Type D (long stay) or Schengen Type C (short stay).

📄 PHASE 2: CORE REQUIREMENTS

  • Work Visa: Job offer + Work permit from Polish employer.
  • Student Visa: Official University admission letter.
  • Family Visa: Sponsor documents from a resident in Poland.

🧾 PHASE 3 & 4: DOCUMENTATION & APPOINTMENT

Prepare your core file: Passport, application form, €30k insurance, and financial proof. Book your biometric appointment through VFS Global Poland.

🧬 PHASE 5 & 6: BIOMETRICS & PROCESSING

Submit fingerprints and photos at VFS. The Embassy verifies employer/university authenticity. Processing takes 10–60+ working days.

📩 PHASE 7 & 8: DECISION & TRAVEL

Approval results in a visa sticker in your passport. Travel to Poland within the validity period and carry all original documents.

🏠 PHASE 9 & 10: REGISTRATION & RESIDENCE CARD

Mandatory: Register your address at the local municipality and get your PESEL number. Apply for the Temporary Residence Card (Karta Pobytu) if staying beyond visa validity.

🔄 PHASE 11 & 12: EXTENSION & PR PATH

After 5 years of continuous legal stay, you become eligible for the EU Long-Term Residence Permit or Permanent Residence in Poland.
