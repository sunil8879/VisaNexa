🇬🇧 UK IMMIGRATION — MASTER END-TO-END PROCESS (2026)

🟢 PHASE 1: IMMIGRATION PATHWAY SELECTION (CORE DECISION)

The UK immigration system is divided into 4 main routes:

1. WORK ROUTES

  • Skilled Worker Visa
  • Health & Care Worker Visa
  • Global Talent Visa
  • Temporary Worker Visas (Seasonal, Creative, Charity, etc.)

👉 Purpose: Work + possible settlement

2. STUDY ROUTE

  • Student Visa (Tier 4 / Student Route)

👉 Purpose: Education + post-study work pathway

3. FAMILY ROUTE

  • Spouse / Partner Visa
  • Child Visa
  • Parent Visa (limited cases)

👉 Purpose: Family reunification + settlement

4. SETTLEMENT / IMMIGRATION ROUTE

  • Indefinite Leave to Remain (ILR)
  • British Citizenship
  • Ancestry Visa (legacy route for Commonwealth countries)

🟡 PHASE 2: ELIGIBILITY CHECK (UK CORE SYSTEM)

UKVI checks 5 pillars:

  • 1. Identity verification: Valid passport, biometric identity verification, UKVI account registration.
  • 2. Genuine purpose: Must match visa type (study → Student, job → Skilled Worker, family → spouse).
  • 3. Financial requirement: Maintenance funds, sponsor income (family), salary threshold (work), tuition fees (students).
  • 4. English language: IELTS UKVI or approved test; exemptions for some nationals/qualifications.
  • 5. Suitability: Criminal record check, immigration violations history, false documents screening.

🟠 PHASE 3: DOCUMENT PREPARATION (FULL CORE FILE)

  • Identity: Passport (valid), passport photos.
  • Civil: Birth certificate, marriage certificate (if applicable).
  • Financial: Bank statements (28–90 days typical), salary slips, sponsor documents.
  • Job/Edu: Degree certificates, CoS (Work), CAS (Students).
  • Immigration: IELTS UKVI, relationship proof (family), biometrics.
  • Additional: Tuberculosis test certificate (India mandatory), police clearance certificate.

🔵 PHASE 4 & 5: APPLICATION & BIOMETRICS

1. Create UKVI account -> 2. Select visa type -> 3. Fill online form -> 4. Upload documents -> 5. Pay fee + IHS -> 6. Book biometrics appointment at VFS Global India (VAC).

🟤 PHASE 6 & 7: UKVI ASSESSMENT & CHECKS

Reviews: CoS/CAS authenticity, salary compliance, job role (SOC code), education credibility, relationship genuineness (family), and TB clearance. May include credibility interviews or employer verification calls.

🟠 PHASE 8, 9 & 10: DECISION, CONDITIONS & ENTRY

Outcomes: Approved, Refused, or Further Info Required. Grant includes validity and restrictions. Entry: Border Force inspection and biometric check at UK border.

🟣 PHASE 11 & 12: LIVING IN UK & EXTENSIONS

Compliance: Must work for sponsor, attend classes, or live with sponsor. Pathways: Skilled Worker → 5 years → ILR; Student → Graduate visa → Skilled Worker → ILR; Spouse visa → 5 years → ILR.

🟢 PHASE 13 & 14: ILR & CITIZENSHIP

ILR (Permanent Residence): 5 years residence + Life in the UK test + English. Citizenship: 12 months after ILR + Life in UK test + ceremony.

⚫ MASTER FLOW (UK IMMIGRATION SYSTEM MAP)

Eligibility → Visa selection → Online application (gov.uk) → Biometrics → UKVI assessment → Decision → Entry into UK → Work/Study/Family stay → Extension → ILR (Permanent Residence) → Citizenship

🧮 VIEW UK POINTS-BASED IMMIGRATION SYSTEM (2026)

🇬🇧 UK POINTS-BASED IMMIGRATION SYSTEM (2026)

Mainly used for Skilled Worker and Health & Care Worker Visas. You must score a minimum of 70 points to be eligible.

🟢 1. MANDATORY POINTS (50 POINTS FIXED)

These requirements are non-negotiable.

Requirement Detail Points
Job Offer Offer from an approved UK employer (CoS) 20
Skill Level Job must be RQF Level 3 or higher (Skilled) 20
English Minimum IELTS B1 or equivalent 10

🔵 2. TRADEABLE POINTS (20 POINTS REQUIRED)

You must gain an additional 20 points through salary or education.

💰 SALARY THRESHOLDS

Annual Salary Condition Points
£38,700 or higher Standard Threshold (2026) 20
£34,000 – £38,699 Partial points 10
Shortage Occupation Lower salary allowed for specific roles 20

🎓 EDUCATION (PHD)

Qualification Relevance to Job Points
PhD Relevant to the role 10
PhD (STEM) STEM field and relevant to role 20

🧮 FINAL UK POINTS FORMULA

Total Points = Job Offer (20) + Skill Level (20) + English (10) + Salary OR PhD (20) = 70 REQUIRED

🎯 KEY SYSTEM DIFFERENCES

  • No Ranking: Unlike Australia, UK is not a competition. If you hit 70, you are eligible.
  • No Pool: There is no waiting pool (EOI). You apply directly once you have a job offer.
  • Sponsor Mandatory: You cannot apply without a Certificate of Sponsorship (CoS).
