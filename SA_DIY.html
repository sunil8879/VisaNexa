Saudi Arabia: DIY vs AGENCY TRUTH

🚨 THE SAUDI LABOUR LAW REALITY

Under Saudi Labour Law, the EMPLOYER is legally responsible for all recruitment costs, including the visa and flight. If an agency in India is charging you ₹2 Lakhs to ₹5 Lakhs for a "Visa," they are likely violating Saudi law and exploiting your lack of information.

1. End-to-End Immigration Lifecycle
Technical Glossary:

• MHRSD: Ministry of Human Resources (The body that approves your work contract).
• Qiwa: The digital platform where all Saudi employment contracts are managed.
• Iqama: Your mandatory Residency Permit issued after you land in Saudi.
• VFS Tasheer: The official agency in India that handles visa stamping for Saudi Arabia.

🏢 Agency Workflow

Step 1: Profile MarketingAgency evaluates your trade (Technician, Engineer, Nurse) and matches you with a "Visa Supplier."Time: 1 Week
Step 2: Internal RecruitmentThey organize "Interviews" at hotels. They charge you a fee just to attend these recruitment drives.Time: 2-3 Weeks
Step 3: Contract SigningYou sign a physical paper in India. Agencies often hide the actual digital contract on the Qiwa portal.Time: 1 Week
Step 4: Medical Exam (GAMCA)They send you to specific clinics. You pay the fee, but they often add a "Handling Charge."Time: 1 Week
Step 5: Enjaz/Mofa FilingThe agency handles the online Enjaz payment. You are rarely told the real cost of this ($10.50).Time: 3 Days
Step 6: VFS StampingThey take your passport to VFS Tasheer. You are charged ₹50k+ for a service that costs ₹10k.Time: 1-2 Weeks
Step 7: DepartureThey book the flight and give you the ticket. The price is usually inflated in your "Package."Time: 1 Week
Step 8: Iqama SupportThe agent "coordinates" with the Saudi PRO to get your ID. You are now tied to that sponsor.Time: 1 Month Post-arrival

🧾 DIY Workflow

Step 1: Direct Job SearchYou apply via GulfTalent, Bayt, or LinkedIn directly to Saudi HR.Time: 1-3 Months
Step 2: SCE/Medical RegistrationEngineers register with Saudi Council of Engineers. You control your professional credentials.Time: 1 Week
Step 3: Digital Contract (Qiwa)You receive and accept your contract digitally on Qiwa.sa. You see your REAL salary and rights.Time: 3 Days
Step 4: GAMCA MedicalYou book your own appointment via Wafid (GAMCA) for approx ₹5,000. No extra bribe.Time: 1 Week
Step 5: Direct MOFA FilingThe employer pays the visa fees in Saudi. You verify the Visa Power (Wakala) online for free.Time: 2 Days
Step 6: VFS Tasheer DirectYou book your own appointment at Tasheer. You pay the official stamping fee (~₹10k) yourself.Time: 1 Week
Step 7: Flight BookingYou (or your employer) book the flight directly. You choose the airline and the timing.Time: 1 Day
Step 8: Self-Managed IqamaYou receive your Iqama from your HR department. You know your rights under the 'Labour Relation Initiative'.Time: 2 Weeks

3. Timeline & Critical Risks

TIMELINE

• Job Hunting: 2-4 months (Direct applications)

• Visa Stamping: 7-10 working days

• Iqama Issuance: Within 90 days of entry

Note: Saudi visa processing is one of the fastest for Indians. Do not believe agents who say "Processing takes 6 months."

THE "FREE VISA" SCAM

• Azad Visa: Selling you a visa for a boss who doesn't have a real job for you. (Illegal and dangerous).

• Kafala Trap: Selling your sponsorship to a company that withholds salary.

• Contract Fraud: Promising high salary in India, but different salary on Qiwa.

5. Final Verdict

CONS of AGENCY
