🇿🇦 GENERAL PROCESS FOR SOUTH AFRICA IMMIGRATION

🧭 1. DETERMINE YOUR IMMIGRATION PATHWAY

Identify your category: Visitor (Short-stay), Study, Work (Critical Skills, General, or ICT), Business/Investment, or Family/Spousal. Each category has distinct requirements under the South African Immigration Act.

📩 2. CHECK ELIGIBILITY & HEALTH REQUIREMENTS

Confirm you meet the criteria for your chosen visa. Important: Long-term visas (over 3 months) require a Medical Report and a Radiological Report (chest X-ray for TB) from a registered physician.

📄 3. DOCUMENT PREPARATION

Prepare your core file: Valid Passport, Application forms, proof of financial capacity, and a Police Clearance Certificate (PCC) from every country where you resided for 12+ months since age 18.

🧬 4 & 5. SUBMISSION VIA VFS GLOBAL & BIOMETRICS

In India, applications are submitted through VFS Global. You must book an appointment, submit your physical file, and provide biometrics (fingerprints and photo). You pay both the visa fee and the VFS service charge.

⚖️ 6. DHA ASSESSMENT & VERIFICATION

The Department of Home Affairs (DHA) in South Africa assesses the application. They verify the authenticity of your job offer, university admission, or family relationship. Processing takes 5–15 days for visitors and 4–8 weeks for work/study visas.

✅ 7 & 8. APPROVAL & PORT OF ENTRY

If approved, you receive a visa sticker or an e-visa. Upon arrival at a South African Port of Entry (Airport), an immigration officer verifies your visa and issues an Entry Permit defining your legal stay duration.

🔄 9. COMPLIANCE & 60-DAY RENEWAL RULE

Maintain legal status by following your visa conditions. If you need an extension, you must apply via VFS at least 60 days before your current visa expires to avoid being declared "undesirable."

🌍 10. PERMANENT RESIDENCY (PR) PATHWAY

After holding a work visa for 5 years (or immediately for certain Critical Skills/Business categories), you may apply for Permanent Residency. PR status allows you to live and work without a specific sponsor.
