🇧🇭 GENERAL IMMIGRATION PROCESS FOR BAHRAIN (2026)

🧭 1. IDENTIFY THE PURPOSE OF ENTRY

First step is to decide your immigration category:

  • Tourism / Visit
  • Business travel
  • Work / Employment
  • Investor / Business setup
  • Student admission
  • Family joining / dependent
  • Medical treatment
  • Transit or short-term entry

👉 Your purpose decides the visa type and documents required.

📌 2. CHOOSE THE CORRECT VISA TYPE

Based on purpose, select: eVisa, Work visa (LMRA-based), Investor visa, Student visa, Family visa, or Government / diplomatic visa.

🔍 3. CHECK ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS

Authorities check: Passport validity (min 6 months), Profession, Financial stability, Sponsorship availability, and Clean immigration history.

🤝 4. ARRANGE SPONSOR (IF REQUIRED)

Many Bahrain visas require a sponsor: Employer (Work), Family member (Dependent), University (Student), Company (Business), or Hospital (Medical).

📄 5. PREPARE REQUIRED DOCUMENTS

Common documents: Valid passport, Photos, Application form, Return ticket, Hotel/Accommodation proof, Bank statements, Invitation letter, and Medical/Police certificates for long-term stays.

🏛️ 6. APPLY FOR VISA

🌐 ONLINE (EVISA SYSTEM)

Most tourist, visit, GCC, and business visas are processed fast via the Bahrain government portal.

🏢 SPONSOR SUBMISSION (LMRA / NPRA)

Work, dependent, and investor visas are applied for by the employer or sponsor on your behalf.

💳 7. PAY VISA FEES

Payment occurs online for eVisas or is handled by the employer/sponsor for work-related visas.

🛡️ 8 & 9. REVIEW, VERIFICATION & ISSUANCE

Agencies (NPRA, LMRA, MOFA) verify identity and security. Approved eVisas are sent via email; Work visas include an entry permit, and Residency is linked to the CPR card system.

✈️ 10 & 11. TRAVEL & RESIDENCY REGISTRATION

On arrival: Immigration checks and biometric verification. For long-term stays: Medical test in Bahrain, Biometrics registration, and CPR (Identity card) issuance.

⚖️ 12 & 13. STAY, COMPLIANCE & RENEWAL

Follow visa purpose strictly. Maintain valid residency and report job changes. Short-term visas must exit before expiry; Work/Family visas are renewable via NPRA/LMRA.

🧠 SUMMARY FLOW: Purpose → Visa Type → Sponsor → Documents → Application → Approval → Travel → Immigration → Residency
