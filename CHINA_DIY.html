China Work Visa: DIY vs AGENCY TRUTH

🚨 THE CHINESE SPONSORSHIP REALITY

In China, the Z-Visa (Work Visa) is strictly tied to a specific employer. Agencies often charge ₹3L to ₹8L for "China Job Packages"—in reality, they are just acting as recruiters for schools or factories. If an agent uses fake documents to get your Notification Letter, you risk immediate arrest and a permanent ban from China during the mandatory police registration.

1. End-to-End Immigration Lifecycle
Technical Glossary:

• SAFEA: State Administration of Foreign Experts Affairs (The body that issues Work Permit Notifications).
• Notification Letter: The official barcoded document required to apply for a Z-Visa in India.
• Class A/B/C: The tiered talent classification (A: High-end, B: Professional, C: Low-skilled).
• Foreigner’s Residence Permit: The actual "visa" you live on, applied for within 30 days of landing.

3. Timeline & Critical Risks

TIMELINE

• Job Hunting: 2-5 months (Direct apps)

• Work Permit Notification: 15-25 working days

• Visa Stamping: 4-7 working days

Note: Chinese visa processing is extremely fast once you have the Notification Letter. Any agent claiming "processing takes 6 months" is likely waiting for a fake job spot.

THE "TEACHING" SCAM

• Tourist Entry: Agents telling you to enter on an L-Visa and work. This leads to immediate JAIL and deportation in China.

• Illegal Schooling: Placing you in a school that doesn't have the license to hire foreigners.

• Degree Forgery: Providing a fake degree for Class B status; caught 100% of the time by the Ministry.
