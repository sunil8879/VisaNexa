🇳🇱 GENERAL PROCESS FOR NETHERLANDS IMMIGRATION

🧭 1. CHOOSE YOUR PURPOSE OF STAY

Decide your reason for moving: Work (Highly Skilled Migrant/Blue Card), Study (Residence Permit), Business (Startup/Freelance), or Family Reunion.

🔍 2. CHECK ELIGIBILITY & VISA TYPE

Consult the Immigration and Naturalisation Service (IND) to verify requirements like age, income, and education for your specific category.

🤝 3. FIND A RECOGNIZED SPONSOR

Most long-term visas require a sponsor (Employer, University, or Family member). Check if your sponsor is in the official IND public register.

📑 4. APPLY FOR RESIDENCE PERMIT & MVV

Applications are submitted via the IND portal. If staying over 90 days, you usually require an MVV (Long-Stay Visa / Type D) to enter the country.

📅 5. BOOK APPOINTMENT (VFS GLOBAL)

Visit VFS Global to submit biometrics (fingerprints and photo) and your passport for the MVV sticker.

⏳ 6 & 7. PROCESSING & DECISION

Standard processing takes 2 weeks to 3 months. Upon approval, receive your MVV and travel to the Netherlands within 90 days.

🇳🇱 8 & 9. AFTER ARRIVAL (MANDATORY STEPS)

  • Collect Permit: Pick up your residence permit from an IND office.
  • Register at Municipality: Register with the Gemeente to get your BSN (Citizen Service Number).
  • Health Insurance: Take out mandatory Dutch health insurance.
  • TB Test: Complete a tuberculosis test if required for your nationality.

🔄 10, 11 & 12. STAY, RENEWAL & PERMANENT RESIDENCE

Follow visa conditions strictly. Renew before expiry. After 5 years of continuous legal residence, you can apply for PR or Dutch Citizenship.
