UAE WORK VISA: DIY vs AGENCY TRUTH

🚨 THE UAE REALITY CHECK

The UAE does not have a "Points Based" PR system. 99% of residents are on Temporary Work Visas. Legally, the EMPLOYER must pay for all visa costs. If an agency is charging you ₹5 Lakhs for a "Dubai Job," they are likely pocketing the money or bribing a small business owner to hire you.

1. Comprehensive Immigration Process
Technical Glossary:

• MOHRE: Ministry of Human Resources and Emiratisation (Handles Work Permits).
• GDRFA / ICP: The bodies that issue the physical Residence Visa stickers and IDs.
• Kafala System: The legal framework where your residency is "sponsored" by your employer.
• Emirates ID: Your mandatory national identity card required for everything in UAE.

🏢 Agency Workflow

Step 1: Document ScrutinyAgency checks if your degree is attested for the UAE market.Time: 2-3 Days
Step 2: CV DistributionThey blast your CV to "partnered" recruitment firms in Dubai. Many claims of "direct links" are fake.Time: 1-4 Weeks
Step 3: Interview ArrangementThey coach you on "Dubai Standards" and arrange a Skype/Zoom interview.Time: Varies
Step 4: Offer Letter IssuanceYou receive a MOHRE Standard Offer Letter. Agencies often charge a "Placement Fee" here.Time: 1 Week
Step 5: Work Permit ApplicationThe employer/agency files the permit. You are kept in the dark about the actual visa category.Time: 5-7 Days
Step 6: Entry Permit (eVisa)The agency sends you a PDF entry permit to board your flight from India.Time: 48-72 Hours
Step 7: Medical & BiometricsUpon arrival, a "PRO" (Agency rep) takes you for a blood test and eye scan in Dubai.Time: 1 Week
Step 8: Visa Stamping & IDThe agency handles the Emirates ID processing. You start work immediately.Time: 2 Weeks

🧾 DIY Workflow

Step 1: Direct Job SearchYou use Naukrigulf, LinkedIn, or Indeed UAE to target real vacancies.Time: 1-3 Months
Step 2: UAE-Style CV PrepYou create a "Gulf Standard" CV mentioning your Visa Status (e.g., "In India" or "On Visit Visa").Time: 2 Days
Step 3: The "Visit Visa" StrategyYou apply for a 60-day tourist visa via GDRFA and travel to UAE to give walk-in interviews.Time: 1 Week
Step 4: Genuine OfferA real company interviews you and issues a MOHRE contract. Legally, they pay all fees.Time: 1 Week
Step 5: Official MOHRE StatusYou check your visa status directly on the MOHRE app using your passport number.Time: Instant
Step 6: "In-Country" ChangeYour employer converts your visit visa to a work visa without you leaving the UAE.Time: 3-5 Days
Step 7: Direct MedicalsYou visit a Govt Medical Center (DHA) yourself. No middleman needed.Time: 1 Day
Step 8: Emirates ID DeliveryYou track your ID via the ICP website and receive it via Empost directly.Time: 10 Days

3. Timeline & Critical Risks

TIMELINE

• Job Hunting: 1-3 months (Optimal stay on visit visa)

• Visa Issuance: 48 hours to 7 days (Digital)

• Onboarding: Within 14 days of offer

Note: UAE has the world's fastest work visa processing. Any agency claiming it takes "months" is lying.

AGENCY FRAUD RISKS

• Salary Bait: Agency promises 5,000 AED; employer pays 1,500 AED.

• Visit Visa Trap: Sending you on a tourist visa and then disappearing.

• Passport Theft: Illegal retention of passports by agents/employers.
