🇨🇳 CHINA IMMIGRATION – GENERAL PROCESS (2026)

🧭 1. CHOOSE YOUR VISA TYPE

Purpose must match visa exactly: Tourist (L), Business (M), Work (Z), Student (X), Family (Q/S), or Talent (R).

📩 2. GET REQUIRED INVITATION / PERMIT

Most China visas require a sponsor. Obtain a Work Permit Notification (Work), JW201/JW202 forms (Student), or a formal Invitation Letter from a Chinese company or resident.

📄 3 & 4. PREPARE DOCUMENTS & FILL COVA FORM

Complete the China Online Visa Application (COVA) form. Prepare passport, biometric photos, and financial proof.

📅 5 & 6. SUBMIT APPLICATION & BIOMETRICS

Submit at the Chinese Embassy or Visa Application Service Center (CVASC). Fingerprints are mandatory for most applicants.

⏳ 7 & 8. PROCESSING & DECISION

Authorities verify invitation and background. If approved, a visa sticker is affixed to your passport.

✅ 9 & 10. TRAVEL & POST-ARRIVAL REGISTRATION

Register your address within 24 hours of entry. For Z and X1 visas, convert the visa into a Residence Permit within 30 days.
