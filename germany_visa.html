🇩🇪 GERMANY IMMIGRATION – GENERAL PROCESS (2026)

🧭 1. DECIDE YOUR VISA TYPE

First step is choosing the correct category:

  • Work Visa (Skilled Worker / Blue Card)
  • Student Visa
  • Job Seeker Visa
  • Family Reunion Visa
  • Business / Self-Employment Visa
  • Training / Internship Visa

👉 This decides your documents, salary requirement

  • Training / Internship Visa

    • 👉 This decides your documents, salary requirement, and processing time.

    🔍 2. CHECK ELIGIBILITY

    Germany checks mainly:

    • Qualification (degree / skill recognition)
    • Job offer (for work visas)
    • University admission (for student visa)
    • Financial proof (blocked account / sponsor)
    • Language requirement (A1–B2 depending on visa)

    📄 3. PREPARE DOCUMENTS

    Common documents include: Valid passport, Visa application form (VIDEX), Biometric photos, degree certificates, CV (Europass format), job offer/admission letter, proof of funds (blocked account), and health insurance.

    📅 4 & 5. APPOINTMENT & SUBMISSION

    Book appointment at the German Embassy / Consulate or VFS Global India. At the appointment: Biometrics (fingerprints + photo), document submission, and occasional interview.

    ⏳ 6. VISA PROCESSING

    Authorities verify job/admission authenticity, financial stability, security background, and qualification recognition.

    • Work visa: 6–12 weeks
    • Student visa: 6–12 weeks
    • Blue Card: 4–10 weeks (faster)

    ✅ 7, 8 & 9. DECISION, TRAVEL & RESIDENCE PERMIT

    Upon approval, receive a visa sticker. After arrival in Germany: Register address (Anmeldung) within 14 days and apply at the Foreigners Office (Ausländerbehörde) to convert your visa into a residence permit card.

    🧠 SUMMARY FLOW: Choose visa → Prepare documents → Book appointment → Submit visa → Wait decision → Travel → Register → Get residence permit
