🇦🇺 AUSTRALIA IMMIGRATION — MASTER END-TO-END PROCESS (2026)

🟢 PHASE 1: IMMIGRATION PATHWAY SELECTION (CORE SYSTEM)

Australia immigration is divided into 5 main streams:

1. TEMPORARY VISAS

  • Visitor Visa (Subclass 600)
  • Student Visa (Subclass 500)
  • Temporary Work Visas
  • Working Holiday Visa (417 / 462)

👉 Purpose: Short-term stay

2. SKILLED MIGRATION (POINTS-BASED PR SYSTEM)

  • Subclass 189 (Skilled Independent)
  • Subclass 190 (State Nominated)
  • Subclass 491 (Regional Skilled Work)

👉 Purpose: Permanent Residency (PR)

3. EMPLOYER-SPONSORED VISAS

  • Subclass 482 (Temporary Skill Shortage Visa)
  • Subclass 186 (Employer Nomination Scheme – PR)

👉 Purpose: Job-based migration

4. FAMILY & BUSINESS VISAS

  • Partner Visa (820/801, 309/100), Parent Visa, and Child Visa.
  • Global Talent Visa (subclass 858).

🟡 PHASE 2: ELIGIBILITY CHECK (AUSTRALIA CORE FILTER)

Department of Home Affairs checks:

  • 1. Identity: Valid passport and biometrics.
  • 2. Match: Purpose must match the visa category.
  • 3. Skills: Occupation must be on the SOL, MLTSSL, or Regional list.
  • 4. English: IELTS / PTE Academic minimum scores.
  • 5. Points: Based on age, education, English, experience, and partner points.
  • 6. Suitability: Medical, chest X-ray, and police clearance.

🟠 PHASE 3: DOCUMENT PREPARATION (CORE FILE)

  • Identity: Passport and photos.
  • Civil: Birth and marriage certificates.
  • Education: Degrees, transcripts, and skills assessment (PR).
  • Work: Employment letters, reference letters, and payslips.
  • Immigration: EOI, invitation, or nomination approval.

🔵 PHASE 4 & 5: ONLINE SYSTEM & SKILLED FLOW

1. Create ImmiAccount -> 2. Select Subclass -> 3. Fill form -> 4. Upload & Pay. For PR: Skills Assessment -> EOI (SkillSelect) -> Invitation to Apply (ITA).

🟤 PHASE 6 & 7: EMPLOYER & STATE NOMINATION

Employer Route: Sponsorship approval -> Job nomination -> Visa application. State Route: Choose State -> Check list -> Submit State EOI -> Receive Nomination.

🟠 PHASE 8 & 9: ASSESSMENT & RFI

Home Affairs verifies identity, skills, sponsorship legitimacy, points score, and health. They may request additional documents (RFI) or skills reassessments.

🟢 PHASE 10 & 11: DECISION & ENTRY

Outcomes: Granted, Refused, or Delayed. Entry: Australian Border Force (ABF) inspection and entry permission at the airport.

🟣 PHASE 12 & 13: COMPLIANCE & PR PATHWAYS

Compliance: Follow visa conditions and work in nominated occupation. PR Pathways: Points-based (189/190/491), Employer (186), Study (485 -> Skilled), or Global Talent.

🟢 PHASE 14 & 15: PR & CITIZENSHIP

PR: Permanent stay, Medicare access, and family sponsorship. Citizenship: 4 years legal stay + 1 year PR + Citizenship test + English + ceremony.

⚫ MASTER FLOW (AUSTRALIA IMMIGRATION MAP)

Eligibility → Skills Assessment → EOI (SkillSelect) → Invitation → Visa Application → Medical + Police Checks → Decision → Entry into Australia → Temporary stay → PR pathway → Permanent Residency → Citizenship
